How to Play SpellBee

Game Rules

  • Create words using the available letters
  • Center letter must be used in every word
  • Words must be at least 4 letters long
  • Find pangrams for bonus points

Scoring System

  • 4 letters: 1 point
  • 5 letters: 5 points
  • 6+ letters: Points equal to word length
  • Pangrams: 7 bonus points

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a pangram?

A pangram is a word that uses all seven letters in the puzzle. Finding pangrams earns you bonus points!

Are proper nouns allowed?

No, proper nouns, hyphenated words, and offensive terms are not accepted in the game.

How often are new puzzles available?

New puzzles are available daily at midnight local time. Each puzzle is carefully curated to ensure an engaging experience.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes! SpellBee is fully optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers. Play anywhere, anytime!

How do I track my progress?

Your daily progress is automatically saved, and you can view your statistics, including total points, words found, and achievement badges.
